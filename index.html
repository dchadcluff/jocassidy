About

Hi, I'm Jo Cassidy — a YA thriller author who loves all things creepy. Bates Motel. Stranger Things. Criminal Minds. Prodigal Son. These are a few of my favorite things.

My thrillers span paranormal, psychological, and contemporary — stories designed to keep you up past midnight with the lights on.

I grew up in Yorba Linda, California, and now live in Utah with my husband and our very suspicious cats.

Jo Cassidy is the pen name of Sara Jo Cluff.
